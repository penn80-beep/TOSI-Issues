Background

SCBAs — Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus — are the air tanks and masks worn by firefighters when entering smoke-filled or hazardous environments. They are critical life-safety equipment. The Sullivan's Island Fire Department is due for replacement SCBAs, and Town Council is weighing whether to purchase them now or delay the purchase. Residents are being asked whether they would prefer the town wait before making this expenditure.
